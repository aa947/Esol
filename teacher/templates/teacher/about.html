About the project


Learning languages is a senseitive issue, specially for us as displaced persons.

This logic was not envented by me, our teacher use this idea in our class but it consumes at least 10 minutes each lession to organise the work groups.

So I automated this process here in this project.

when it comes to learn the language we have to put a pressure on our students to learn fast.

The idea of this project is to organise the class groups in an English class in such a way that there are not Two students with the same mother language in one group, so the students are obligated to speak in english to connect with each other.

the secon idea is to shuffel the students' groups throug the semester, so approximately you will work with evrey single students in your class who doesn't talk your mother languge, so you will learn the different accsents and deal with them.

So 10 mins for evrey class each lession means if the class held 3 times a day, you loose 30 mins. and if you have 10 classes in this school you loose 5 hours every day. and if you have more and more schools in the country, you can imagine what we loose.
